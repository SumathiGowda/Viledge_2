Water-Related Complaints

Facing water service issues in your area? You can register complaints in the following situations:

  • Unreliable or irregular water supply
  • Water contamination or poor water quality
  • Lack of access to clean drinking water
  • Water wastage due to leaking pipes or taps
  • Unhygienic water storage or distribution systems
